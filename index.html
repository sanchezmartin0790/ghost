SIMON RILEY

Lieutenant Simon "Ghost" Riley is a British special forces operator and a cornerstone member of the elite multinational unit Task Force 141. Recognizable by his iconic skull-patterned balaclava and tactical headset, Ghost is revered for his deadly efficiency, his skill in close-quarter combat, intelligence reconnaissance, and an air of mystery that surrounds him.

The making of GHOST

Born Simon Riley in Manchester, England, Ghost endured a brutal childhood marked by abuse and hardship. Those early years forged his iron will and pushed him toward the military, where he would transform into one of the most feared operators in British special forces.

Task Force

Ghost serves as a Lieutenant under Captain Price in Task Force 141, an elite multinational unit operating outside conventional command. His role spans close-quarters combat, intelligence gathering, and covert reconnaissance across the globe's most dangerous conflict zones.

The Betrayal

During Operation Kingfish, Ghost and Roach were ambushed and killed by General Shepherd after recovering critical intelligence. Shepherd, seeking to cover his tracks, betrayed Task Force 141 — a moment that sent shockwaves through the entire MW2 story.

THE REBOOTED LEGACY

In the 2022 reboot of Modern Warfare II, Ghost returned with a deeper backstory and a more human side. His bond with Soap MacTavish became a fan favorite, cementing his status as one of gaming's most iconic and beloved characters of all time.
